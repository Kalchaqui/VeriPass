Hello everyone, I'm going to present **VeriScore**.

VeriScore is a B2B on-chain credit scoring infrastructure that, through a database layer and AI agents, provides verifiable credit scoring to Web3 financial institutions, enabling them to offer collateral-free on-chain credit services similar to traditional banks and credit card companies.

**Built for the Cronos x402 Paytech Hackathon**, VeriScore leverages Cronos EVM, the x402 payment protocol, and Crypto.com AI Agent SDK to deliver a complete credit scoring solution.

---

### **El Problema (45 segundos)**

The first problem is that exchanges have very little information about the real user. They cannot verify a user's identity the way banks do, and they cannot assess whether a user is able to repay a loan. This is because there is no verifiable credit history in Web3; data is fragmented across platforms, and exchanges cannot audit credit information. There is also no standardized scoring system, which means exchanges cannot make informed lending decisions and face a high risk of default.

Additionally, existing solutions lack integration with AI agents for automated monitoring and don't provide accessible data interfaces for AI assistants to query credit information in real-time.

---

### **La Soluci√≥n - VeriScore (60 segundos)**

The solution becomes much simpler with **VeriScore** because it is an on-chain credit scoring infrastructure that allows exchanges and banks to assess their users' credit risk, enabling them to offer USDT or other cryptocurrency loans without collateral.

**Key Features:**

1. **x402 Micropayments on Cronos EVM**
   - Operates through a pay-per-use model with x402 micropayments
   - Real payments verified on-chain on Cronos Testnet
   - No contracts or approvals needed - instant access

2. **On-Chain Verification**
   - Verifiable blockchain data through smart contracts deployed on Cronos EVM
   - Three contracts: IdentityRegistry, CreditScoringMini, and VeriScoreSBT
   - All credit scores and identity data are transparent and auditable

3. **AI Agent Integration**
   - Crypto.com AI Agent SDK for automated score queries and monitoring
   - MCP Server exposing credit scoring data to AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude)
   - Automated workflows for credit assessment

4. **Crypto.com Ecosystem Integration**
   - Crypto.com Market Data MCP for enhanced scoring with market data
   - Real-time token prices and historical data
   - Complete integration with Cronos ecosystem

**What Exchanges Get:**
- Query credit scores in real time
- Verify on-chain identity and credit history
- Make lending decisions based on transparent and auditable data
- Access credit information via AI agents and MCP Server
- Automated monitoring and alerts
